I. The objective of the survey

The luminance distortions for images have been well studied in research. However, much less attentions have been focused on images that suffer from geometric distortions.

In this survey, you will rate the image quality for distorted images. The survey lasts for about 15 min. Your effort is well appreciated, because this will contribute to a novel algorithm which prevents such time-consuming tasks in the future. 😄


II. What's my part in it?

Well, we will ask you to rate the image quality for the distorted images according to the following criterion:

  • geometrical distortion
  • content coverage
  • overall image quality considering the above two criterion
