The objective of the survey

The luminance distortions for images, such as blurring and Gaussian noises, have been well studied in research. However, much less attentions have been focused on images that suffer from geometric distortions.

In this survey, you will rate the image quality for distorted images. The survey lasts for about 15-20 min. Your effort is well appreciated because what you are doing contributes to a novel algorithm that prevents the similar time-consuming tasks in the future. 😄


What's my part in it?

Well, we will ask you to rate the image quality for the distorted images with respect to the reference image. You will rate the quality according to the following criteria:

  • geometrical distortion
  • content coverage
  • overall image quality considering the above two criterion
